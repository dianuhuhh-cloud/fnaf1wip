WELCOME TO FREDDY FAZBEAR’S PIZZERIA

ABOUT THE GAME

You're an employee at the newly established Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. The animatronics move dangerously at night. Use cameras, doors, and lights with limited power to survive five nights. Each night gets harder. They don't like being watched, stay alert and plan carefully.

CHARACTERS

FREDDY

Only moves when not being stared at in the monitor. Attacks from the right door and when the power runs out to 0%.

DISCOVER

ROLES

NIGHTGUARD

Monitor security cameras, manage limited power usage, and strategically defend your office while surviving until 6 AM as the animatronics roam the halls.

JANITOR

The janitor maintains the hallways of the pizzeria by cleaning up the messes that the kids left behind while strange sounds echo through the building.

ENGINEER

Restore failing systems and respond to unexpected technical malfunctions to keep the pizzeria operational throughout the night, all while staying alert to the subtle signs of trouble lurking in the halls.

POLICEMAN

The policeman is an externally hired entity by Freddy Fazbear's investigating the disappearances of the missing children that happened that one night while assisting the night guard during his duty.
